Frequently Asked Questions

How do students update their ongoing project work?

Students must navigate to the "Update" section within their allocated project task to provide progress reports and submit any related files or documents.

How can I reset my password if I forget it?

If you forget your password, simply click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page of the Capstone portal. Enter the email address linked to your account, and you'll receive an email with instructions on resetting your password. Follow the provided link, set a new password, and confirm the changes. Afterward, you can log in to your account using your updated credentials.

Where can I see notifications on Capstone Portal?

You can see notifications on the student dashboard session.

Can I see my project progress report?

Yes. You can see both your progress bar as well as your grades on Capstone Portal.
